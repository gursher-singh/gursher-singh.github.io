What I do

Manage enterprise endpoints and modern device provisioning using Microsoft Intune and Windows Autopilot, and administer identity systems with Entra ID and Active Directory.

Specialize in Tier 2 troubleshooting and root-cause analysis - restoring services fast and eliminating the conditions that caused the issue in the first place.

Across all of it, I prioritize clear documentation, reliable support, and keeping production environments stable.

My Approach

Every incident is a signal, not just a task. When something breaks repeatedly, it means a process is missing, a policy is misconfigured, or documentation never existed. I fix the root cause, document it, and make sure the next person has what they need to handle it without escalating. I think reliable IT is something that's built intentionally, not something that just happens.
